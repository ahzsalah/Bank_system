Our Loans

Personal Loan

Interest Rate: From 5.99% APR

Term: 1 to 5 years

Personal loans can be used for any personal expenses and are not tied to a specific purpose. They are typically unsecured, which means they are not backed by a collateral like a house or car.

Car Loan

Interest Rate: From 3.99% APR

Term: 1 to 7 years

Car loans are specifically designed for the purchase of a car. The car serves as collateral for the loan, which often results in a lower interest rate compared to unsecured loans.
